About Us

FROM THE CHAIRMAN

This year has most certainly been an unusual year. We have all learnt to adapt, relearn and overcome unforseen obstacles. The pandemic and the lockdown has taught us lifelong lessons in adaptability and positivity. Tilak Maharashtra Vidyalaya too has been adapting to the newer operational methods and began to provide online education for all classes from the beginning of our regular academic year. It has been a learning process for both the staff and the students and we have been successful in the smooth functioning of the online curriculum delivery and conducting co curricular activities till date. CAPABILITY TO COPABILITY is the new mantra for everyone which has been adapted enthusiastically by Tilak Maharashtra Vidyalaya too. The school has conducted several activities online like celebration of Yoga day, online eco friendly Ganpati making workshop, story telling sessions. We have also launched the our online newsletter this year. The Vidyalaya has always had a compassionate outlook towards the society. Given the present pandemic and lockdown situation, the Vidyalaya organised a donation drive called “Gift a Diwali”for Diwali. The drive received a good response and we were able to contribute to making the festival joyous for just above 250 families. The school has received accolades from the Science Olympiad foundation and has been recognised as the Best Holistic school award 2019-20. The Vidyalaya has also been honoured by the ELETS foundation in the World Education Summit 2020 for Exemplary contribution to Education sector. The construction of the new school building is now complete and the entire staff of Tilak Maharshtra Vidyalaya is waiting to welcome our students into the new premises. As always, we have planned a year full of creative, productive and stimulating academic and co curricular activities for our students in the coming year. We are eagerly waiting to meet our students and are working steadily and surely towards our goal of excellence in all aspects of the school. I wish all of you good health, prosperity and a safe and happy new year! Dr. Pranati. R. Tilak
Chairman
Tilak Maharashtra Vidyalaya
